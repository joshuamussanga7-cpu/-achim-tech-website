Privacy Policy for TapEscape

Last Updated: April 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to TapEscape. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information Collection and Use

TapEscape does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, location, or microphone.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking software.

3. Local Data Storage

The game saves your progress (High Scores, Gem counts, and selected Player Color) locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data remains on your phone and is not transmitted to any external servers. If you uninstall the app, this data will be deleted by the Android system.

4. Third-Party Services

Currently, TapEscape does not use third-party services (such as Ads or Cloud Saves) that may collect information used to identify you.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our game is safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we are fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

6. Permissions

The app may require the following permissions for gameplay purposes only:

  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback during collisions or game events.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
