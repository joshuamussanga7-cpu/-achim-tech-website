Privacy Policy for Achim Calu

Last updated: April 2024

ACHIM Business and Management Consulting Services ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Achim Calu mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Offline Functionality

Achim Calu is a completely offline application. It does not require an internet connection to function. All calculations are performed locally on your device, and no data is ever sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

2. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, device identifiers, location data, or usage statistics. Since the app operates entirely offline, there is no technical mechanism for us to collect any user data.

2. Permissions

The app does not require any special permissions (such as camera, contacts, or storage) to function as a scientific calculator.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that collect information used to identify you.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
